## Our Mission
The goal of **Universe** is to bring organization and ease to the development of roleplay game developers. (although the ecosystem includes features that can be applied to several other types of games, too)

## Notable Features
**Universe** makes use of a modular system with a layout similar to the following:
* Weather
* Phone
* Vehicles
* Houses
* Trading
* Inventory
* Pets

These features are the highlight of our ecosystem, but there are many other small modules in our system as well.
